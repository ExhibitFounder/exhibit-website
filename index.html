How It Works

Post. Rate. Repeat.
It's that simple.

01

Snap your fit

Open the camera — body detection frames you perfectly. Add a song from Spotify, tag your brands, and post to Friends, Exhibit, or Nearby.

02

Rate the fit

Swipe through outfits. Upvote the ones that go hard, downvote the misses. Double-tap to rate, comment to talk fashion, save fits you love.

03

Build your streak

Post daily, build your streak. Each fit lives for 24 hours — one active fit at a time. Your archive becomes a private lookbook of your style evolution.

Music Integration

Every outfit has a soundtrack

Attach a song from Spotify to your daily fit check. When someone scrolls to your post, your 15-second preview plays automatically — your outfit, your vibe, your music.
