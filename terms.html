5. The One-Active-Fit Rule

Exhibit operates on a "one active fit" model, which is a core feature of the App:

  • Each user may have only one active (publicly visible) outfit post at any time.
  • When you post a new outfit, your previous active post is automatically archived from public feeds.
  • Active posts expire after 24 hours and are automatically archived.
  • Archived posts remain accessible only to you in your private archive.
  • This behavior is intentional by design and cannot be overridden.

6. Feeds & Content Discovery

The App provides three content feeds for discovering outfits:

  • Friends Feed: Shows active posts from users you follow who also follow you back (mutual friends).
  • Exhibit (For You) Feed: A personalized feed that ranks and surfaces content based on your engagement patterns, including your voting history, viewing behavior, and content preferences. This personalization is powered by a taste profile that is automatically generated and updated as you use the App.
  • Nearby Feed: Shows active posts from users in your geographic area (approximately 25-mile radius), based on your device's location data.

When posting, you can choose which feeds your outfit appears in (Friends, Exhibit, Nearby, or any combination). Feed algorithms and ranking may change at any time without notice as we improve the App.

7. Social Interactions

The App enables the following social features:

  • Following: You may follow other users. When two users follow each other, they become mutual friends.
  • Voting: You may upvote or downvote posts. Votes influence content ranking in the Exhibit feed and contribute to a poster's upvote count displayed on their profile.
  • Comments: You may post comments on other users' active posts. Comments must comply with our Content Guidelines. We reserve the right to remove comments that violate these Terms.
  • Posting Streak: The App tracks consecutive days on which you post an outfit. Streaks are displayed on your profile and reset if you miss a day.

9. Location Services & Passport

Certain features of the App require access to your device's location:

  • Nearby Feed: Uses your device's GPS to show posts from users in your geographic area. Location data is encoded as a geohash for privacy and queried at reduced precision (approximately city-level).
  • Passport: Allows you to virtually set your location to browse the Nearby feed in other areas. Passport locations are stored locally on your device and are reduced to approximately 1.1 km precision.
  • Post Location: If you post to the Nearby feed, your post's approximate location is stored to enable geographic discovery.

You may disable location permissions at any time in your device settings, but the Nearby feed and related features will not function without location access. For more information on how we handle location data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
